# Penny Lane -- Savings & Beyond

The budget tracker works. Now we want to help users see into the future.

Add a savings projection that shows how their money will grow (or shrink) over
the next 12 months, accounting for the fact that income and expenses don't stay
flat. Users should be able to play with assumptions and see the impact
instantly.

After that, build one more feature of your choosing -- something that makes
this app genuinely useful for someone tracking their finances.
