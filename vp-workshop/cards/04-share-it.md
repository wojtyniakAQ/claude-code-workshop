## Make a deck from your work

Paste this:

> Turn the key findings from the app into a 5-slide presentation as a single HTML file I can flip through with arrow keys -- clean, one big idea per slide. Then show it to me.

Use the arrow keys to flip through. That's a presentation, built from your real
analysis, in under a minute.

Prefer a one-pager? Ask for that instead:

> Turn the key findings into a one-page executive summary I can email.

## The honest truth about slides

- Claude Code is **excellent** at HTML decks and reports like this one -- they
  render instantly and look sharp.
- For a **native PowerPoint in your corporate template**, it gets you maybe 70% of
  the way and then you polish the rest by hand. So when you truly need a `.pptx`
  for a meeting, ask Claude to use **Marp** (it writes plain text and exports to
  PDF or PowerPoint), or export this and tidy it up.
- For speed and wow, HTML wins.

## Why this beats redoing it in chat
Your deck was built from the same `/refresh-report` pipeline you made in Step 3.
Next month: drop in new files, press the button, regenerate the deck. A chat would
start from a blank page every single time.
