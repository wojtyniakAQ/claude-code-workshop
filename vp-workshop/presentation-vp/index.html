Now that you've felt it

How it works

01

A coworker, not a search box

Opinionated. Can't read your mind. Great with clear instructions, and it adjusts when you give feedback. Treat it like a sharp new hire — be specific, and tell it when something's off.

02

It works in your files

Everything it makes is a real file in your folder that you own. Not trapped in a chat — email it, keep it, open it next year.

03

Tell it once — it remembers

It remembers the preferences you give it. Say it in plain English and it sticks — there's no settings file to open.

Just say this
From now on, always use our brand color — a warm amber — as the main accent in anything you build. Remember that.

Ask for the next thing — the amber shows up without being reminded.

04

If it drifts, just remind it

Long session? You don't manage anything. If it ever loses the thread, tell it in plain words — "we've covered a lot, keep going from here" — the way you'd nudge a colleague.

05

You're in control

It asks before doing anything real. Nothing happens that you didn't approve. It will not break your laptop.
