Notes from the data team (read me before using these files!)
===========================================================

These three files are monthly exports, but they come from three different
systems and they do NOT line up cleanly. A couple of things to know:

1. The column names drift between months. For example one month calls it
   'play_count' and another calls it 'plays'; March calls the date column
   'day' instead of 'date'. They mean the same thing.

2. The date formats are all different (2025-02-03 vs 02/03/2025 vs
   03-Mar-2025). Same dates, different dressing.

3. MARCH IS MISSING ITS FIRST WEEK (days 1-7). There was a system
   migration, so anything before March 8th just isn't there. That's
   expected, not a bug.

4. IGNORE ANY ROW WHERE source = TEST. Those are QA artifacts from our
   testing, not real listens. Leave them out of every total.
